Welcome to the EddieHub Newsletter page!

EddieHub is an Open Source community focusing on reciprocal collaboration between members of the tech community. The aim of the community is to encourage and promote communication, best practices and technical expertise in an inclusive and welcome environment.

Each month we showcase what the community has been up to, top tips and shout outs.

If you would like to receive our Newsletter, fill out the form below!

Pets in tech

This feature showcases our most loyal pair programming furry friends!

It has been proven that having a pet helps with your mental health. They provide companionship and serve as a reminder to take a break, look away from the screen and be more active. This is so important when you have been staring at the screen for hours - and will probably help you be more productive. Send us a photo of your pet next to (or on!) your tech equipment by completing the form below. Don't forget to let us know your pet's name and a fun fact about them.

Community achievements

The members of the EddieHub community do amazing things!

This is the opportunity to bring to our attention an achievement by one of our community members who you think deserves a shout out. This could be the first time they raised a pull request, maybe they landed the job of their dreams, completed a course or started a YouTube channel. If you can think of someone who has done something amazing this month then complete the form below. Don't forget to let us know their social handle!

Community video/blog of the month

Have you come across a video or a blog that really inspired you this month from one of the community members?

Then this is the place to let us know! You might have learnt something new, got the incentive to approach a challenge differently or the content might have given you that 'aha!' moment. If you think the EddieHub community needs to see this content, then complete the form below. Don't forget to give us the link!
